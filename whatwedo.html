It's not just about delivering products and services—it's about creating meaningful connections that shape the future of commerce.

The bedrock of what we do is connecting millions of buyers and sellers, making everyday life easier with innovative solutions that truly work for you.

Through our e-commerce marketplace, financial services, logistics, and B2B solutions, we are breaking down barriers, building connections, and driving growth across industries.

Konga Marketplace

Konga.com offers a dynamic marketplace that connects millions of buyers and sellers across Nigeria.

Our platform provides a seamless shopping experience for customers, whether they’re purchasing everyday items or luxury goods.

With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction, we offer fast delivery, secure payments, and a reliable return policy, making shopping on Konga not just convenient but enjoyable.

Since our launch in July 2012, we have been working to revolutionise commerce and trade across Africa. Our commitment to customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do, powering us to provide unparalleled convenience and exceptional service through features like our Same-day delivery, 7-day Free Return Policy, real-time Order Delivery Tracking, and round-the-clock Customer Support.

As we expand our mall, we're excited to power more variety and convenience, constantly innovating to create a seamless shopping experience tailored to our customers' needs.
