About

  1. Home
  2. About

Our Vision is to be the engine of commerce and trade in Africa.

Our Mission is to create a trusted and vibrant retail ecosystem that facilitates trade across Africa - connecting millions of buyers and sellers while empowering everyone to grow, thrive, and succeed.

To achieve this, we are committed to enabling buyers, sellers, and businesses to access best priced products, transact from anywhere, and grow their business with ease.

Our Core Values

Underpinning our vision are six core values. These six values, embedded across the Group, are the foundation of our corporate culture.

Love for our customers
Satisfied customers are essential to our success. There are no kings in Konga, only servants of the customer.
Hardwork
We embrace hard work. We understand that it is only through pushing ourselves beyond our limits of comfort that we become better as individuals and as a company.
Teamwork & Sacrifice
We work together disregarding personal interests to meet the needs of our customers and to build a stronger company.
Honour & Integrity
We are open, honest and trustworthy in all our dealings.
Passion
We are passionate about our brand and our mission.
Constant Evolution
We always question the norm looking for opportunities to birth new ideas. As individuals we embrace flexibility and rapid change.

Our Strategy

We have a winning strategy focused on building the most trusted tech-driven ecosystem that facilitates commerce and trade in Africa.

Our strategy is designed to connect our customers and sellers, bridge the barriers to market access, and empower businesses to unlock new opportunities for the future. At the foundation of our strategy are four clear strategic pillars.

Create Africa’s most trusted eco-system for commerce
Drive industry leading customer-centric innovations
Bridge market access barriers
Power business growth
